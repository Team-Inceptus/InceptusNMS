Enum Class Difficulty

public enum Difficulty
Represents a Minecraft Difficulty. Difficulties are used to indicate how hard the game is to play.

  • Enum Constant Summary

    Enum Constants
    Enum Constant
    Description
    PEACEFUL
    Peaceful difficulty. No hostile mobs spawn and the player's hunger is disabled.
    EASY
    Easy difficulty. Hostile mobs spawn but do not deal as much damage as they would on other difficulties.
    NORMAL
    Normal difficulty. Hostile mobs spawn and deal normal amounts of damage.
    HARD
    Hard difficulty. Hostile mobs spawn and deal more damage than they would on other difficulties.
